Club World Cup Draft 2025

Compete with friends by picking clubs from the groups. Here's the idea:

  • Pick one team from each group.
  • A win gives you 3 points; a draw gives 1 point.
  • If an opponent picked the other team, you score the same points again for each of them.

Your total from every match decides the winner.

Scoring Example

2 - 1

Two others picked Al-Hilal . Real Madrid wins:

  • Win points: 3
  • Bonus: 3 points × 2 = 6
  • Total for this match: 9 points
1 - 1

If it ends in a draw with the same two opponents :

  • Draw points: 1
  • Bonus: 1 point × 2 = 2
  • Total for this match: 3 points
