Club World Cup Draft 2025

Welcome!

Ready for some footy fun? Pick your favourite clubs and see if you can outscore your colleagues! ⚽🎉

📋 How to Play

  • ✍️ Choose one team from every group.
  • 🚀 If your pick wins, you earn 3 points plus 3 bonus points for each other player who backed the opposing club.
  • 🤝 A draw gives 1 point plus 1 bonus per opposing player.
  • 🏆 Highest score after all matches wins!

Example

You pick Real Madrid and two colleagues pick Al-Hilal.

2 - 1

Real Madrid wins:

  • Win points: 3
  • Bonus: 3 × 2 = 6
  • Total: 9 points

If it had been a draw:

  • Draw points: 1
  • Bonus: 1 × 2 = 2
  • Total: 3 points
