Welcome to the Club World Cup Draft

This draft lets you compete against friends by picking clubs from the Club World Cup groups. Here is how it works:

  • Pick one team from every group.
  • When your team plays a match, you earn 3 points for a win or 1 point for a draw.
  • You get bonus points when other players picked the team your club is playing against:
    • Win: +3 points for each opposing player who chose the other team.
    • Draw: +1 point for each opposing player who chose the other team.

The total from all of your club's matches decides your final score. The player with the most points after the tournament wins!

Scoring Example

2 - 1

Two other players picked Al-Hilal. Real Madrid wins:

  • Win points: 3
  • Bonus: 3 points × 2 players = 6
  • Total for this match: 9 points
1 - 1

If the same match ended in a draw:

  • Draw points: 1
  • Bonus: 1 point × 2 players = 2
  • Total for this match: 3 points
